PDF Tools Pro is a completely free, browser-based PDF utility suite. Every operation — merging, splitting, compressing, searching, watermarking, rotating, and more — runs entirely on your device using modern JavaScript libraries.

Built with: pdf-lib, PDF.js, Bootstrap 5, Font Awesome, and pure JavaScript.

Mission: To give everyone access to powerful document tools — completely free, with zero compromise on privacy.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. No File Uploads

All PDF processing on PDF Tools Pro occurs entirely within your web browser. Your files are never sent to any server. We have no technical capability to access, read, or store your documents.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage only to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) and a short list of recently processed file names (not the file content). You can clear this anytime using the "Clear History" button.

3. No Personal Data

We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifiable information. No account or registration is required.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We may use third-party advertising (e.g. Google AdSense) which may use cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

5. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (e.g. Google Analytics or Plausible) to understand aggregate usage — which tools are used most. This data is anonymized and cannot identify individuals.

6. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@pdftoolspro.com
